\sect{Theorem}:
Let $\{u_1,u_2,...,u_p\}$ be an orthogonal basis for a subspace W. $\forall y \in W$ the linear combination:
\[
  y=c_1u_1+c_2u_2+...+c_pu_p
\]
is given by the equation:
\[
  c_i=\frac{y \cdot u_i}{u_i \cdot u_i}=\frac{y \cdot u_i}{||u_i||^2}
\]
